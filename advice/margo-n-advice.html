Accountability Matters

When you begin to work through the curriculum, you may be tempted to hold yourself accountable to a certain work pace. If you're like me, you'll soon find that this is a mistake.

You can't know how long it will take you to work through each lesson. Additionally, you want learning, not timing, to be your highest value.

You can solve this problem by committing to a certain amount of time each week. In this way, if you put in the time, you are a success...no matter how quickly you move through the material!

In the end, you will have truly learned the material at the right pace for YOU. Good luck!
